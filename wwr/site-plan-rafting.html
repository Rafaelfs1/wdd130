What is the minimum age to participate in rafting activities?

The minimum age to participate in rafting activities is typically 12 years old, but it may vary depending on river conditions and company policies. Be sure to check age restrictions before booking your adventure.

Is prior rafting experience necessary to participate in the activities?

Prior rafting experience is not necessary to participate in our activities. Our experienced guide teams will provide instructions and guidance before the activity to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants.

What are the difficulty levels of the rafting trips offered?

We offer a range of rafting trips catering to different skill levels and experiences. From gentle family-friendly floats to thrilling adventures for the more experienced, we have options for all types of adventurers.
