Exciting Rafting Trips

River Rapids Adventure

Experience the thrill of a lifetime on the River Rapids Adventure. Navigate through Class III and IV rapids, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Perfect for adrenaline junkies and experienced rafters.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Highlights: Stunning landscapes, challenging rapids, professional guides
  • Price: $85 per person

Family Fun Float

A perfect trip for families and beginners, the Family Fun Float offers a leisurely ride down gentle rapids. Enjoy the serene environment and make lasting memories with your loved ones.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Highlights: Safe for children, picturesque views, wildlife spotting
  • Price: $45 per person

Sunset Serenity Paddle

Unwind with our Sunset Serenity Paddle. This peaceful trip takes you through calm waters, offering a unique perspective as the sun sets. Ideal for couples and those seeking relaxation.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy to Intermediate
  • Highlights: Beautiful sunset views, tranquil atmosphere, experienced guides
  • Price: $70 per person
